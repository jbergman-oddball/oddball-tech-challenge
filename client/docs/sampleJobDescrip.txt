What you'll be doing:

You’ll contribute to both front-end and back-end development of form-related services and features on VA.gov. You’ll help modernize legacy form infrastructure, optimize user experience, and ensure your work is performant, testable, and compliant with 508 accessibility and security standards.

Experience working with cloud platforms (AWS preferred)
Understanding of accessibility best practices and web standards (WCAG, Section 508)
Strong communication and collaboration skills in Agile environments
